# Play Store Listing – English

## App name
Diary

## Short description (max. 80 characters)
Personal diary – local, private, no cloud required.

## Full description (max. 4000 characters)
Diary is a simple, fast app for capturing personal log entries – no sign-up, no subscription, no third-party servers.

**Quick entry**
Open the app → write → save. No required fields except the text itself. Optionally add mood and health on a 5-point scale, GPS location, categories and tags.

**Everything stays on your device**
Entries are stored locally in a SQLite database. No account required, no telemetry, no ads.

**Backup on your own server**
Connect the app to your own Nextcloud server for backups. Data goes directly there – never to servers run by the app developer. Optional AES encryption before upload.

**Security**
Protect your entries with biometrics (fingerprint / face recognition) and a fallback password. The app locks automatically after 15 seconds in the background. A recovery code can be set up for emergencies.

**Search & filter**
Full-text search with colour-highlighted matches. Filter by categories, tags and time range (Today / Week / Month / All).

**Statistics**
Entry count, streak record, mood and health trend curves, activity heatmap, and top categories and tags – for day, week, month, year or a custom range.

**Calendar view**
Monthly calendar with marked entry days. Tap a day to see its entries.

**Daily reminder**
Optional local notification at a time you choose – no push server, everything local.

**Language**
German and English. Switch in settings.

---
No ads. No account. No cloud. Your data belongs to you.
